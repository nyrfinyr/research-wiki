# Łukasz Kaiser

Ricercatore di deep learning. Al momento di [[vaswani-2017-attention]] era a [[google-brain]] (email `lukaszkaiser@google.com`).

## Cosa sappiamo

- Coautore (equal contribution) di **Attention Is All You Need** (NeurIPS 2017) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1].
- "Spent countless long days designing various parts of and implementing tensor2tensor, replacing our earlier codebase" (insieme a [[aidan-gomez]]) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1 footnote].
- Lavori precedenti citati nelle reference: "Can active memory replace attention?" (NIPS 2016) e "Neural GPUs learn algorithms" (ICLR 2016) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf refs 16, 17].

## Relazioni

- Affiliazione (2017): [[google-brain]].
- Tool: [[tensor2tensor]] (co-implementatore).
