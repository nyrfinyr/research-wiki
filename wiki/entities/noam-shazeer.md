# Noam Shazeer

Ricercatore di deep learning. Al momento di [[vaswani-2017-attention]] era a [[google-brain]] (email `noam@google.com`).

## Cosa sappiamo

- Coautore (equal contribution) di **Attention Is All You Need** (NeurIPS 2017) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1].
- "Proposed scaled dot-product attention, multi-head attention and the parameter-free position representation"; "became the other person involved in nearly every detail" [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1 footnote].
