# Ashish Vaswani

Ricercatore di deep learning. Al momento di [[vaswani-2017-attention]] era a [[google-brain]] (email `avaswani@google.com`).

## Cosa sappiamo

- Primo autore (con equal contribution e listing order random) di **Attention Is All You Need** (NeurIPS 2017) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1].
- "Designed and implemented the first Transformer models" insieme a [[illia-polosukhin]]; "crucially involved in every aspect of this work" [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1 footnote].
