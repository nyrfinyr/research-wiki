# Jakob Uszkoreit

Ricercatore di deep learning. Al momento di [[vaswani-2017-attention]] era a [[google-research]] (email `usz@google.com`).

## Cosa sappiamo

- Coautore (equal contribution) di **Attention Is All You Need** (NeurIPS 2017) [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1].
- "Proposed replacing RNNs with self-attention and started the effort to evaluate this idea" — idea seminale del [[transformer]] [source: raw/papers/vaswani-2017-attention.pdf p.1 footnote].
