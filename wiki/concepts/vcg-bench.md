# VCG-Bench

VCG-Bench (Video-ChatGPT Benchmark) is an open-ended video QA benchmark that evaluates video-LLMs along dimensions such as correctness of information, detail orientation, contextual understanding, temporal understanding and consistency, generally with GPT-assisted scoring.

In the wiki it is one of the standard benchmarks used in works on visual token pruning and mechanistic analysis.
