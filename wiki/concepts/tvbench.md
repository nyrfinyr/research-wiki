# TVBench

TVBench (Cores et al. 2024) is a video question-answering benchmark designed to minimize static-scene bias: it includes tasks such as Action Antonym, Action Sequence, Scene Transition, Moving Direction, Object Count, where answering correctly truly requires looking at the temporal evolution of the video.

In the wiki it is the primary benchmark used by Map-the-Flow for mechanistic analysis of [[temporal-reasoning]].
