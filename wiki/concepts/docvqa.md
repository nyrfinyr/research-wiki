# DocVQA

DocVQA is a question answering benchmark on document images (scans, forms, reports) in which the model must read the text in the page and answer natural-language questions. It requires robust OCR and reasoning over the document's structure.

In the wiki it is used as a standard bench for the "document understanding" and text-rich VQA dimensions.
