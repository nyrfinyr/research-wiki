# mPLUG-Owl

Open-source MLLM from Alibaba DAMO (Ye et al., 2023): ViT vision encoder + visual abstractor + LLM, two-stage training (alignment + instruction tuning). Founder of the mPLUG-Owl series (later mPLUG-Owl2, mPLUG-Owl3).
