# MMMU-Pro

MMMU-Pro is the harder version of [[mmmu]], obtained by filtering questions solvable by text-only models and adding visual distractor options, so as to force actual reliance on the image modality. It reduces linguistic shortcuts and therefore better separates models that actually reason about the visual.

In the wiki it is listed among the "image / multidisciplinary" benchmarks used in reports of generalist MLLMs.
