# Mistral

Open-source LLM family from Mistral AI (Jiang et al., 2023+). Mistral-7B (September 2023) introduces Grouped-Query Attention and Sliding Window Attention; Mixtral-8x7B adds MoE. Backbone used in MLLMs (best VideoChat2 variant) and as the textual LLM in long-video QA pipelines.
