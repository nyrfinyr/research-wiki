# InfographicVQA

InfographicVQA is a VQA benchmark on infographics, i.e. images with complex layouts that combine text, icons, charts and illustrations. The questions require integrating visual and textual information distributed across the entire image, and thus stress both OCR and spatial reasoning.

In the wiki it is cited as a benchmark complementary to [[docvqa]] and [[chartqa]] for evaluating document understanding.
