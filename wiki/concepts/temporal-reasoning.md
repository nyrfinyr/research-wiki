# Temporal Reasoning

Temporal reasoning is the sub-task of [[temporal-understanding]] that requires combining information from multiple frames to infer a fact not readable from any single frame (action sequences, causality, counting over time). It is the target capability of mechanistic-analysis work on video-LLMs.

In the wiki it is the primary task investigated by Map-the-Flow on [[tvbench]] and is connected to [[fine-grained-video-understanding]].
