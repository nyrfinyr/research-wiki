# LLaMA

Family of open-weights LLMs from Meta (Touvron et al., 2023+): LLaMA, LLaMA 2, LLaMA 3. Transformer decoder architecture with RoPE, SwiGLU and RMSNorm; scales from 7B to 405B. Fundamental backbone for most open-source MLLMs (LLaVA, MiniGPT-4, Video-LLaMA via Vicuna) and textual LLM baseline.
