# RefCOCO

RefCOCO is a referring expression grounding benchmark built on COCO images: given a natural-language expression referring to a specific object, the model must localize its bounding box. It is the de facto standard for measuring an MLLM's language-region "linking" ability, along with its variants RefCOCO+ and RefCOCOg.

In the wiki it appears as a [[grounding]] evaluation benchmark in MLLM reports and in work on visual attention.
