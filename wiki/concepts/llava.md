# LLaVA (family)

Large Language and Vision Assistant — family of open-source MLLMs based on visual instruction tuning, started by Liu et al. (2023). Minimal architecture: ViT (typically CLIP/SigLIP) + MLP projector + LLM (Vicuna/Mistral/Qwen). Progenitor of a long lineage (LLaVA-1.5, LLaVA-1.6/NeXT, LLaVA-OneVision, LLaVA-Video, LLaVA-NeXT-Video) used as backbone in many later papers.
