# Qwen (family)

Family of open-source LLMs from Alibaba (Bai et al., 2023+). Trajectory: Qwen → Qwen1.5 → Qwen2 → Qwen2.5 → Qwen3, with scales from 0.5B to 235B (including MoE variants). Widespread LLM backbone in modern MLLMs (LLaVA-OneVision, LLaVA-Video, VideoLLaMA 3 use Qwen2/Qwen2.5).
