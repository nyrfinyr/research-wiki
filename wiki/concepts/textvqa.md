# TextVQA

TextVQA is a question-answering benchmark on natural images containing scene text (signs, labels, packaging) where answering correctly requires reading the text present in the image. It measures the combined visual perception and OCR capability of an MLLM.

In the wiki it appears as a text-rich comparison benchmark, alongside [[ocrvqa]] and [[docvqa]].
