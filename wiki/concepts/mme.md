# MME

MME (Multimodal Evaluation) is an image-VQA benchmark that evaluates MLLMs on 14 sub-tasks of perception and cognition (existence, count, position, OCR, commonsense, code reasoning, etc.) with yes/no questions, reporting an aggregate score per category. It is one of the most cited image benchmarks as a reference in video-LLM and generalist MLLM work.

In the wiki it is cited as a reference image benchmark alongside [[mvbench]] and [[mmmu]].
